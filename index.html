About

Enterprise Scale. DTC Speed.

Your product descriptions make perfect sense to customers. But when someone asks ChatGPT "What's the best [your category]," AI might describe your $200 product identically to a $40 generic.

I've managed product content at both ends: Walmart (100M+ users, massive catalog complexity) and DTC brands (small teams, launch speed). Whether it's thousands of SKUs or your first 10 products, I've been there.

8 years optimizing for conversion across Shopify, Amazon, and retail channels. Coordinating launches. Making content work at every touchpoint.
